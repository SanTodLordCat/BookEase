About This Project



Problem Statement


During peak hours, students face long queues at campus cafeterias. The high volume of customers causes delays and may lead to incorrect orders being recorded by cafe staff.



Proposed Solution


UniCafe Smart Ordering System provides an online ordering platform that allows students to browse menus, place orders, select pickup times or delivery locations, and make payments conveniently.



Benefits


  • Reduce queue congestion.
  • Reduce ordering mistakes.
  • Improve student experience.
  • Faster food ordering process.
  • Better cafe operation efficiency.
